About ShopLedger

ShopLedger is a complete point‑of‑sale, inventory, and bookkeeping system built specifically for Nigerian small stores – provision shops, supermarkets, boutiques, and more.

We believe that every store owner deserves professional tools without paying enterprise fees. That’s why ShopLedger works offline, installs like an app, and gives you real‑time sales & stock visibility – all from your phone.
